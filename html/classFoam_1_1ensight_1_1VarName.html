Detailed Description

Specification of a valid Ensight variable-name.

space !#%()*+,-./;@[]^

If spaces exist, they must be quoted

Variable names cannot start with a digit. The maximum part name length (in GUI: 49 chars) - not addresssed. The maximum variable length (in GUI: 49 chars) - not addresssed.
