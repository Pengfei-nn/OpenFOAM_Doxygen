Detailed Description

Low Reynolds-number k-kl-omega turbulence model for incompressible flows.

Reference: 

    Standard model:
        Walters, D. K., & Cokljat, D. (2008).
        A three-equation eddy-viscosity model for Reynolds-averaged
        NavierStokes simulations of transitional flow.
        Journal of Fluids Engineering, 130(12), 121401.
        DOI:10.1115/1.2979230

    Typo corrections:
        Furst, J. (2013).
        Numerical simulation of transitional flows
        with laminar kinetic energy.
        Engineering MECHANICS, 20(5), 379-388.

The default model coefficients are 

    kkLOmegaCoeffs
    {
        A0             4.04
        As             2.12
        Av             6.75
        Abp            0.6
        Anat           200
        Ats            200
        CbpCrit        1.2
        Cnc            0.1
        CnatCrit       1250
        Cint           0.75
        CtsCrit        1000
        CrNat          0.02
        C11            3.4e-6
        C12            1.0e-10
        CR             0.12
        CalphaTheta    0.035
        Css            1.5
        CtauL          4360
        Cw1            0.44
        Cw2            0.92
        Cw3            0.3
        CwR            1.5
        Clambda        2.495
        CmuStd         0.09
        Prtheta        0.85
        Sigmak         1
        Sigmaw         1.17
    }
