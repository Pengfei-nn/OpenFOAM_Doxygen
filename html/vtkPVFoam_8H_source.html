30 Description
31  The backend for the vtkPVFoamReader reader module -
32  providing a paraview reader interface for OpenFOAM meshes and fields.
33 
34  Similar, and sometimes better, functionality may be provided by the
35  native VTK OpenFOAM reader. OpenCFD has recently (2017) been working
36  on improving the native VTK OpenFOAM reader for the benefit of everyone.
37 
38  In some areas the reader module lacks compared to the native reader
39  (notably the ability to work on decomosed datasets), but provides
40  additional handling of sets,zones,groups. Some features have also since
41  been adapted to the native reader. Additionally, the reader module
42  provides a useful platform for testing new ideas.
43 
44 Note
45  The reader module allows two levels of caching. The OpenFOAM fvMesh
46  can be cached in memory, for faster loading of fields. Additionally,
47  the translated VTK geometries are held in a local cache. The cached
48  VTK geometries should incur no additional overhead since they use
49  the VTK reference counting for their storage management.
