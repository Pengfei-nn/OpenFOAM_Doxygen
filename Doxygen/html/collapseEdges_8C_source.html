29 Group

32 Description
33  Collapses short edges and combines edges that are in line.
34 
35  - collapse short edges. Length of edges to collapse provided as argument.
36  - merge two edges if they are in line. Maximum angle provided as argument.
37  - remove unused points.
38  - collapse faces:
39  - with small areas to a single point
40  - that have a high aspect ratio (i.e. sliver face) to a single edge
41 
42  Optionally checks the resulting mesh for bad faces and reduces the desired
43  face length factor for those faces attached to the bad faces.
44 
45  When collapsing an edge with one point on the boundary it will leave
46  the boundary point intact. When both points inside it chooses random. When
47  both points on boundary random again.
48 
49 Usage
50  - collapseEdges [OPTION]
