32 Description
33  Correction limiting method based on the relative particle velocity.
34 
35  This method limits the velocity correction to that of a rebound with a
36  coefficient of restitution \f$e\f$. The relative velocity of the particle
37  with respect to the mean value is used to calculate the direction and
38  magnitude of the limited velocity.
