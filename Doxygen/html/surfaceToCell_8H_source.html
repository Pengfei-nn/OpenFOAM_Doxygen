27 Class
28  Foam::surfaceToCell
29 
30 Description
31  A topoSetCellSource to select cells based on relation to surface.
32 
33  Selects:
34  - all cells inside/outside/cut by surface
35  - all cells inside/outside surface ('useSurfaceOrientation', requires closed
36  surface)
37  - cells with centre nearer than XXX to surface
38  - cells with centre nearer than XXX to surface \b and with normal
39  at nearest point to centre and cell-corners differing by
40  more than YYY (i.e., point of high curvature)
41 
42  \heading Dictionary parameters
43  \table
44  Property | Description | Required | Default
45  file | The surface "filename" | yes |
46  scale | surface scaling factor | no | -1
47  nearDistance | Near distance to the surface | yes |
48  curvature | surface curvature selection | yes |
49  outsidePoints| Points outside of surface | yes |
50  includeCut | Include cut cells (bool) | yes |
51  includeInside | Include inside cells (bool) | yes |
52  includeOutside | Include outside cells (bool) | yes |
53  useSurfaceOrientation | useSurfaceOrientation | no | false
54  \endtable
