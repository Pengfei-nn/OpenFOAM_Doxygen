28  Foam::Matrix
29 
30 Description
31  A templated (m x n) matrix of objects of <T>.
32  The layout is (mRows x nCols) - row-major order:
33 
34  \verbatim
35  (0,0)
36  +---> j [nCols]
37  |
38  |
39  v
40  i [mRows]
41  \endverbatim
