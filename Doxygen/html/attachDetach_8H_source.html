27  Foam::attachDetach
28 
29 Description
30  Attach/detach boundary mesh modifier. This modifier takes a set of
31  internal faces and converts them into boundary faces and vice versa
32  based on the given activation switch.
33 
34  The patch is oriented using the flip map in the face zone. The
35  oriented faces are put into the master patch and their mirror
36  images into the slave.
